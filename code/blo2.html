Is banana good for weight loss?

  • admin
  • Lifestyle

 

‘Abs are made in the kitchen’. How many times have you heard or seen this quote while browsing on how to lose belly fat fast?  This holds true, without a proper diet with the proper foods, you will not achieve those six pack abs that you crave so much. But what foods should you consume?

Our digestive system is also known as our second brain, it is at the very center of our core health. Optimal gut bacterial environment and digestive system function is the key to a healthier body. You could be eating the cleanest nutrition rich foods but if your body doesn’t digest it properly, no nutrients are absorbed. Resulting in, fat built up and water retention.

Even if you have a healthy weight, fat around the midsection can impose health risks in the future. 

Belly fat is quite stubborn and cannot be shed off easily. That is why you need to find the right combination of a good workout routine with the proper diet friendly foods which is the fastest way to lose belly fat.

Here is what you must do in order to burn belly fat and stay fit.

Must-Do Things To Lose Belly Fat

Do’s

Why

Exercise 

Aerobic exercise is an effective weight loss method. Studies suggest it is particularly effecting at slimming your waistline and help you burn calories the natural way. Some of these exercises include swimming, jogging, walking, cycling, boxing and rowing. Studies suggest it’s even more effective in combination with strength training  exercise

Healthy Proteins

Protein is a good weight loss agent and it must be consumed wisely in order to control your weight. Some of the foods that contain high amounts of proteins are lentils, nuts, eggs, yoghurt, cheese, milk etc.

Healthy Carbs

A healthy lifestyle begins with a healthy diet that you need to stick to. Make sure you add Soluble fiber may help you lose weight by increasing fullness and reducing calorie absorption. Try to include plenty of high fiber foods in your diet as all of these foods help reduce belly fat. 

Healthy Fats

Fatty fish are rich in protein and omega 3 that can aid in reducing belly fat. 2 to 3 servings of fatty fish per week is recommended that you consume. Some examples of fatty fish include salmon, sardines, mackerel etc. Use coconut oil, as this will decrease the amount of fat in your body.

Keep in mind that losing weight is not about starving yourself or being on a rigorous diet. It’s about including every type of food in your daily diet in generous amounts to stay full and energetic while getting all the healthy nutrients at the same time

Knowing the knowledge is not enough, the application is important. There are no magic solutions to losing belly fat.

All that it requires is some effort, commitment, and perseverance on your behalf.

Successfully adopting some or all of the strategies and lifestyle goals discussed in this article will definitely help you lose the extra pounds around your waist.
